int  Nside

Finds the healpixes neighbouring the given healpix, placing them in the array "neighbour". Returns the number of neighbours. You must ensure that "neighbour" has 8 elements.

Healpixes in the interior of a large healpix will have eight neighbours; pixels near the edges can have fewer.

Finds the healpixes containing and neighbouring the given xyz position which are within distance 'range' (in units of distance of the unit sphere). Places the results in 'healpixes', which must have at least 9 elements. Returns the number of 'healpixes' set.

Returns -1 if "Nside" < 0.

Converts a healpix index, plus fractional offsets (dx,dy), into (x,y,z) coordinates on the unit sphere. (dx,dy) must be in [0, 1]. (0.5, 0.5) is the center of the healpix. (0,0) is the southernmost corner, (1,1) is the northernmost corner, (1,0) is the easternmost, and (0,1) the westernmost.
